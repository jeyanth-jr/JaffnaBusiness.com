About Jaffna Business

Jaffna Business (Pvt) Ltd is proud to be a leading provider of business services in the Northern Province of Sri Lanka. Established with the vision of strengthening the region’s commercial ecosystem, our company offers reliable, affordable, and efficient services to meet the needs of entrepreneurs, startups, and established businesses.

We specialize in a variety of services that make starting, managing, and growing your business easier. From registering your company to ongoing secretarial services and business consulting, we are dedicated to supporting your business at every stage of its journey.

Our team consists of experienced professionals who understand the unique challenges of doing business in Jaffna. With local expertise and a commitment to excellence, we’re here to help your business succeed.
